Lytra is simple ... like a cup of tea

LyTra was created through an appreciation for tradition and a desire to innovate. Weddings and happiness means something different to everyone, so why not have something custom made for you? For us, it started at the moment when a tea ceremony was witness. There was so much culture ingrained in the rituals, all the colours involved in the process, and the tedious practice of following custom. The idea of old and new, coupled with the client's personality is what excited us. Whether it be through photography or wedding planning, we are here to serve you. Living in a world where we get instant gratification, Lytra is here to make that process as simple as possible. Simple... like a cup of tea.
